Maximizing Your Remote Internship Experience

  1. Request a clear scope of work and timeline from your supervisor or onboarding contact.
  2. Block dedicated work hours and actively participate in scheduled virtual meetings.
  3. Use tools like Slack, Teams, or Zoom for prompt communication and collaboration.
  4. Document your accomplishments and updates to share during wrap-up or feedback sessions.
  5. Express interest in continued work or opportunities beyond the internship if you’re performing well.
